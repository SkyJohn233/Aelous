JESUS GARZA SR. - Laredo Morning Times Caption Close Jesus Garza Sr., 71, passed away on Friday, April 27, 2018 at a local hospital. Mr. Garza was born in Laredo, Texas and had been a lifelong resident. He was employed by the City of Laredo Sanitation Department for over 35 years. He will be dearly missed by all who knew him. He is preceded in death by is parents, Carlos and Maria de Jesus Garza; brothers, Francisco Garza and Juan Carlos Garza. He is survived by his loving wife, of 52 years of marriage, Guadalupe R. Garza; children, Jesus (Anselma) Garza, Jr., and Antonio (Maria) Garza; grandchildren, Vanessa (Kristopher Paredes) Garza, Antonio Garza Jr., Emma (Jesse Bay) Castillo, Emily Ann Garza, Amanda Marie Garza and Jesus Garza III; great-grandchildren, Yanessa, Yaretzi, Kristopher and Renata; sister, Juanita Garza; brother, Ramon Garza. Also surviving him are other relatives and many friends. Visitation will be held on Monday, April 30, 2018 from 4 p.m. to 9 p.m. and a Prayer service will be officiated by Rev. Gilberto Velez at 7 p.m. in the Iglesia Cristiana Misericordia, 4519 E. Del Mar Blvd, Laredo, Texas 78041. Religious Service will be held at 10 a.m. on Tuesday, May 1, 2018 at Iglesia Cristiana Misericordia. Funeral services will follow at the Laredo City Cemetery. Services under the direction of the  Hernandez-Lopez and Sons Funeral Chapels, 800 Boston St . at San Bernardo Ave. www.HernandezLopezFH.com
