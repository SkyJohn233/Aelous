ADMISSION | Gulf English Nursery To register for a place at The Gulf English Nursery you must complete the online registration form using the link at the bottom of this page. Once you have registered the school Registrar will contact you to arrange a time for you and your child to visit the Nursery for a Taster Visit. During this Visit one of the Nursery Teachers will observe your child playing and judge their suitability for a place at GEN. We will also ask you some questions about your child and give you a copy of the Home School Agreement. The fee for Taster Visits is  700 QR  and is non-refundable.
