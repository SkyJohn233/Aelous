❤️ Red Heart Emoji A classic love heart emoji, used for expressions of love. Displayed in various shades of red on most platforms. A similar emoji exists for the heart suit  in a deck of playing cards. On  Snapchat , this emoji displays next to a friend when you have been #1 BFs with each other for two consecutive weeks. Note: the Unicode character name  Heavy Black Heart pre-dates color emoji, when the term referred to a solid, black ❤︎ heart character. See the glossary for information on the term black  in Unicode character names.
