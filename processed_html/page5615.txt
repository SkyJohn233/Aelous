Basis Point Asia-Pacific Secondary Loan Composite Loan Pricing LPC has teamed up with major banks in Asia to offer loan investors and portfolio managers weekly valuations for loans in the region. The Secondary Loan Composite focuses on countries with liquid secondary markets including Australia, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Philippines and South Korea. The service builds upon Basis Point s coverage of loan markets in Asia-Pacific. Currently our partner banks include: ABN Amro, Barclays Capital, BNP Paribas, Citigroup, Credit Lyonnais, HSBC, Standard Chartered and WestLB.
