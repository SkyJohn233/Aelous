VTS Operator Course Certificate - International Dictionary of Marine Aids to Navigation VTS -2012-47 VTS  Operator Course Certificate is a document awarded by the training organisation, to indicate that a trainee has achieved successful completion of an accredited  IALA  Model Course i.e.  V -103/1  VTS  Operator Training,  V -103/2  VTS  Supervisor Training,  V -103/3  VTS  On-the-Job Training and  V -103/4 On-the-Job Training Instructor.  This course certificate alone should not be an authorisation to operate.
