Tupac Shakur discography - Wikipedia The  discography of  Tupac Shakur , an American rapper, consists of eleven  studio albums . Four were released before Shakur's death on September 13, 1996, and seven were  posthumously  released, the first being  The Don Killuminati: The 7 Day Theory , released on November 5, 1996 (although the album was finished before Shakur's death).
