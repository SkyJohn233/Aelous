Worker dies after falling into a box compactor at a Macy's store An employee died in an apparent workplace accident Wednesday morning at the Macy's store in the Cross County Shopping Center in Yonkers. Sources tell News 12 Westchester Dimitri Henry became caught in a box compactor and was crushed. Henry was rushed to Jacobi Medical Center in the Bronx where he later died. The incident occurred before the store was open for business. Macy's corporate offices released a statement saying, "The incident in our Cross County store was a tragic accident. Our thoughts and prayers are with Mr. Henry's family and co-workers. At this time, we are conducting an investigation into the situation and working with the authorities." Police and the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) are expected to be back in the store Thursday investigate exactly what happened.
