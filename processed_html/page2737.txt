The couple, who were married in Minneapolis, enjoy splitting their time between their two homes in Naples and Edina. They raised two children, Carolyn and Steven, and have four grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Warren Dahl, 90, grew up in Bricelyn, Minnesota, and is the owner of Ingebretsen's Scandinavian Meat Market in Minneapolis. Arleen Dahl, 88, grew up in Minneapolis and was the head cook for the public school system in Richfield, Minnesota.
