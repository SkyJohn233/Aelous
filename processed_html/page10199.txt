Show Jumping Holstein Stallion Joost Greenacres Stud Holstein Stallion : Joost Stallion Joost The preferent Stallion Joost was a top progenitor of show jumpers  and was highly ranked on the index. He had many international offspring. He has several KWPN approved sons and had a breeding value of 148 The combination of Joost and the mare Abgar became a very famous mix for show jumpers  and included international competitors like : Ommen , VIP , Carrera , Germus R and Palfrenier amongst others.
