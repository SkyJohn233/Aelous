Leadership   Sanskrithi School of Business Leadership at SSB consists of the Governing Boards. The Governing Board comprises people of companies, noted entrepreneurs leading academics and foreign illuminaries, who have devoted time, thought, and resources towards developing the School. Sanskrithi School  of Business (SSB) was founded in 2010  by Mr. Vijay Reddy in the rural regions of Puttaparthi.Driven by his endeavor to develop the rural youth, SSB was established to be a forerunner in the development and creation  of future management leaders..
