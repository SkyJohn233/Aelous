larry raymond montgomery obituary weston wv obittree september 25 1931 october 02 2017 september 25 1931 october 02 2017 larry raymond montgomery 86 of simpson run road weston passed away on monday october 2 2017 at his residence with loving family surrounding him he was born in lewis county on september 25 1931 son of the late arvel montgomery and eula dee snyder montgomery on september 3 1955 he married his wife of 62 years lora workman montgomery who survives larry was blessed with four children steve montgomery and wife heather of jane lew brad montgomery and wife debi of weston mark and devon perrine of bridgeport and 5 great grandchildren he was also survived by one sister jackie mcclain of weston and several nieces and nephews mr. montgomery was retired from consolidated natural gas company as a foreman with 36 years of service he was a veteran of the united states army serving 6 years during the korean conflict and was stationed in japan and germany he was a member of st matthew united methodist church of weston where he had served as a sunday school teacher and usher he was very civic minded and was active as a scout master pack 105 weston city council lewis county business development authority member of the northview umc quartet weston lodge 10 a.f a.m. weston chapter 40 order of eastern star was active with weston jaycees where he coached little league basketball for several years and was the owner of towne shoppe for several years his hobbies included woodworking camping and fishing he will be missed by family and friends family and friends will be
