Tekashi 6ix9ine is  reportedly  facing life in prison after several criminal charges were filed against him in federal court yesterday, November 19. Per  TMZ , the most serious count is discharging a firearm while committing a crime, which carries "a mandatory minimum of 25 years in prison."
