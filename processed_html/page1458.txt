30 Best Animated TV Shows of All-Time - Page 16 The spin-off from  Beavis and Butt-Head ,  Daria  perfectly satirized suburban high school life. Everyone recognized themselves or someone they knew in at least one of the characters, and Daria s sardonic observations on the exaggerated world she was living in were often very insightful. Being on MTV, it was also entertaining when Carson Daly, Bif Naked and Dave Grohl provided guest voices.  Grey s Anatomy  fans will also recognize Sarah Drew as the voice of Fashion Club member Stacy Rowe, beginning with the second half of season one. Animation isn t just for kids anymore (if it ever was). Animated TV series have exploded over the last few decades, giving us some incredibly memorable programs far beyond the average Saturday morning cartoon.
