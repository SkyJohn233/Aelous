Sharad sits down with Sean Clark from SHOEme.ca | The Province Sean Clark, President and Co-founder of Shoeme.ca Published:  December 4, 2014  Updated:  December 4, 2014 7:22 AM PST  Filed Under: Sean Clark is an entrepreneur that is changing the way we look at shoe buying in Canada. As the President and co-founder of  SHOEme.ca , he says that he has a passion for giving his clients an engaging online experience that will keep them coming back.  Sean’s background comes from his success with Vancouver based success story Clearly Contacts where he ran the Australian Business. Hardy Capital is the official backer of this online shoe biz.   With the successful sale of Clearly Contacts,  Roger Hardy  comes on board to SHOEme.ca and acquires American business  OnlineShoes.com . Online commerce is all about convenience with a click of a button.  Sharad got a chance to sit down with Sean and talk about SHOEme.ca and his story as an entrepreneur starting the company in his parent s basement. This story is one to admire and watch as they have experienced triple digit growth since their start.   The world loves their shoes, and Sean and Roger are just getting started with how they service the market place with the coolest brands and the best online experience they continually enhance for the masses.
