Arklay County | Resident Evil Wiki | Fandom Arklay County  is a county somewhere in the Midwestern United States. The county was home of both the  Arklay Mountains  and  Raccoon City , and was the site of a  t-Virus  outbreak in 1998 that eventually led to the  Raccoon City Destruction Incident .  Law enforcement was provided by the  Arklay County Sheriff's Department .
