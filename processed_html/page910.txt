The Plant Stand   Garners Landscaping The Plant Stand is a full service Nursery that specializes in  locally grown plants  &  flowers.  We have over 12 greenhouses located in  Newport where we  grow approximately 90% of our products from seeds, cuttings, or plugs.  We offer bedding plants (annuals  &  perennials), hanging baskets,  vegetable plants, herbs, shrubs, tropicals, palms  &  more! The Plant Stand also carries landscaping materials such as  Long Leaf Pine Straw,  Mulch, Top Soil, Compost, bagged soils, fertilizers,  insect control  &  other hard goods.
