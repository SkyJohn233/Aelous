Copper nanoparticle decorated three dimensional graphene with high catalytic activity for Huisgen 1,3-dipolar cycloaddition - RSC Advances (RSC Publishing) *                                                     Corresponding authors                                                                                                             a                             Faculty of Chemistry, Shahid Beheshti University, Tehran 1983969411, Islamic Republic of Iran                                 E-mail: m-dabiri@sbu.ac.ir A copper nanoparticle decorated three-dimensional graphene nanocomposite was prepared at room temperature by reduction of copper sulfate using  L -ascorbic acid as the reducing agent. The synthesized Cu NPs-3D G nanocatalyst exhibited excellent catalytic activity in the synthesis of 1,2,3-triazoles.
