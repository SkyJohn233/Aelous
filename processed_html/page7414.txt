Rural Training School, Kintampo - Wikipedia The  Rural Training School  is public tertiary health institution in  Kintampo  in the  Brong Ahafo Region  of  Ghana . [ 1 ] [ 2 ]   The school is in the  Kintampo District . [ 3 ]  The activities of the institution is supervised by the  Ministry of Education . The Nurses and Midwifery Council (NMC) is the regulates the activities, curriculum and examination of the student nurses and midwives. The Council's mandate Is  enshrined  under section 4(1) of N.R.C.D 117. [ 4 ]
