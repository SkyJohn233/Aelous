The cemetery is located in the City of Toledo, Lucas County, Ohio, and is # 7064 (Calvary Catholic Cemetery / Calvary) in “Ohio Cemeteries 1803-2003”, compiled by the Ohio Genealogical Society. The cemetery is registered with the Ohio Division of Real Estate and Professional Licensing (ODRE) as Calvary Cemetery with registration license number CBR.0000981939.
