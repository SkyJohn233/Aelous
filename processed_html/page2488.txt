Urban Haat Market in Hazaribagh - Video Reviews, Photos, History | HolidayIQ The Urban Haat is located on the NH 33, at about 3 km from the town of Hazaribagh. This Haat has been the result of the efforts of the Jharkhand Government, which displays and sells works created by the native artisans of Hazaribagh and areas of Jharkhand. Two large shops of handicraft from Jharkhand and six food courts are located within this Haat. The Urban Haat, which is a preferred destination for the art-lovers of the city, is situated close to the AIR Hazaribagh Station and just behind the Hazaribagh Lake. Figures made of terracotta, moulded figures, Sohari paintings, products of leather, handloom garments, furniture and many are sold at this Haat. The stalls inside this Haat have been painted of late with Sohrai, adding an aesthetic look to the place. There is also a canteen located within the area of the Haat. Know cost before you go!
