WCP Theatre Company WCP Theatre Company (or Western College Players) are Plymouth's longest established amateur dramatics (non musical) group. We were formed  90 years ago. We present a wide variety of quality productions  in The Drum, Theatre Royal Plymouth, The Devonport Playhouse and within the local community. We also regularly pr esent plays/ concerts in aid of local charities. We have a  full social programme and welcome members of  ALL AGES Staging  much loved ,  important and/or popular  favourites with  wide appeal  (many of  which are  new, exclusive  productions) our aim is to produce innovative ,  exciting  and  completely unique theatre- which  educates  and  entertains members and audiences alike. We are always looking for new members. For details of how you can get involved in our company- or if you just want more information- you'll find our contact details on the 'contact' page.
