OSHAWA   A world of flavours returns to Oshawa next week. Fiesta Week 2018 is back, running June 17 to 23. The annual multicultural event starts with the traditional Father s Day Parade and Concert on June 17, starting at 12:30 p.m., with the music getting underway in Memorial Park at around 1:30 p.m. The parade begins at Centre and King streets, travels south to Gibb Street, west to Park Road and north to King.
