For a wall switch that operates a switched outlet, a rocker switch guard can prevent constant reprogramming of items like clocks, vcr's and radios. It can protect a switch connected to a dvr or a garage door opener. For a furnace, a switch protector it will keep a switch from accidently being shut off.
