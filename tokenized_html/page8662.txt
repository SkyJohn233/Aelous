what is slow writing twinkl teaching wiki slow writing is an approach to writing that uses a step-by-step structure to create a short text or paragraph similar to a writing frame a teacher will give specific writing prompts or instructions as to what grammar language or punctuation features to include in each sentence for example children could be asked to include a semicolon in a sentence or use a simile in a sentence here are some examples of slow writing prompts giving your children these slow writing prompts lets them focus on a higher quality of work rather than just filling a page with text slow writing may be used with a whole class or as part of an intervention group it is intended to slow down the writing process to ensure high quality improve and up-level children 's writing skills
