This is because your pancreas does not produce any insulin, or not enough, to help glucose enter your body s cells   or the insulin that is produced does not work properly (known as insulin resistance). The Trust s diabetes team offers the following specialist services at the Lister, New QEII and Hertford County hospitals: At the Lister patients are admitted onto one of the inpatient wards as and when required.
