Radiation Oncologists in Austin, TX - Ratings and Reviews | Vitals.com A radiation oncologist is a physician who specializes in the use of nuclear medicine in diagnosing and treating cancer in a patient. These doctors work in concert with other cancer specialists in order to determine the best form of treatment for a specific patient. Radiation oncologists are in charge of determining the correct dose of radiation to be used in treatments like radiotherapy and chemotherapy, as well as the appropriate amount of time that radiation should be administered in order to maximize the therapeutic benefits.
