Included are a 14 page booklet of rules, charts and tables, a 22 "  x 34 "  map of the battlefield, and 158 counters. Units are broken into factions and clans... one player commands the Eastern Army, the other player commands the West.  Extensive rules for Melee Combat, Clan Effectivenes, and Defections are provided. Victory is determined by counting loss of  " Stalwartness Points " .  The first player to drop 160 points due to losses and defections, loses the game.  If the game manages to last eight turns, without either player losing, the Western player is assumed to win. First published in  Command magazine  Issue #23, and later republished in  Command Magazine Japan  #54.
