Shades Death Creek - Wikipedia Shades Death Creek  (also  Shades of Death Creek ) is a stream in  Fayette County, West Virginia , in the  United States . [ 1 ] Shades Death Creek has been noted for its  unusual place name . [ 2 ]  The creek was named for its gloomy and dark course.
