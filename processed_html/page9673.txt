Colman Boyd | Griffith College Colman has over twenty years’ experience lecturing in business studies. He specialises in accounting and finance, and HR Management at Griffith College Dublin and Portobello College. He has also provided expert business support to companies, including those in the pharmaceutical industry, on strategy development, performance management, HRM and finance. Colman has lectured in China and Bosnia-Herzegovina and has represented the Griffith College Business Faculty in promoting relations with the University of Sarajevo.
