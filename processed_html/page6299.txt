Ernst Stavro Blofeld (You Only Live Twice, 1967): 25 best James Bond villains ever | Stuff Bond's arch-enemy had appeared on-screen before, in From Russia With  Love and Thunderball – but it was only with You Only Live Twice that we  got to put a face to the name – and what a face. Austin Powers' parodic  Dr Evil may have robbed his scarred visage of some of its impact, but as  soon as he starts talking in that clipped whisper, you realise Blofeld  is no joke. And he gets some killer lines courtesy of screenwriter Roald  Dahl (yes,  that  Roald Dahl). "Give him his cigarettes. It won't be the nicotine that kills you, Mister Bond."
