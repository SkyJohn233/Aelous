Read Full Biography                                         At the start of their career,  Talking Heads  were all nervous energy, detached emotion, and subdued minimalism. When they released their last album about 12 years later, the band had recorded everything from art-funk to polyrhythmic worldbeat explorations and simple, melodic guitar pop. Between their first album in 1977 and their last in 1988,  Talking Heads  became one of the most critically acclaimed bands of the  ' 80s, while managing to earn several pop hits. While some of their music can seem too self-consciously experimental, clever, and intellectual for its own good, at their best  Talking Heads  represent everything good about art-school punks.
