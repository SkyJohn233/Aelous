Also known as gram flour, organic Chana Besan is made up dried chickpeas that have been ground up into a gluten free four. Organic Chana Besan is commonly used in a batter to make savory kadhi with yogurt or deep fried pakodas and is also useful in combination with other flours or for making deserts. For those seeking a gluten-free flour substitute, Organic Chana Besan can be useful in place of wheat flour in many recipes. It lends a nutty taste to breads, sauces and other foods, and is also a versatile and healthy alternative to complement recipes and dishes. As a certified USDA Organic, non-GMO, vegan and gluten-free product, organic Chana Besan complements vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free lifestyles.
