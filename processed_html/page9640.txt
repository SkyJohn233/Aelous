Bless Online   Neowiz assures global server will not be affected by closure of Korean server | MMO Culture Korean developer and publisher Neowiz recently announced that it will be shutting down its multi-million MMORPG, Bless, on November 20 in South Korea. Powered by Unreal Engine 3, the population of Bless decreased dramatically soon after launch. Even with the massive  Rebuild project  to revitalize the game based on community feedback did not work out. Bless entered Open Beta on January 27 2016, and its run is will end at 2 years and 10 months. The development team  issued a notice for the global server , stating that all resources will now be directed to take Bless Online off Early Access to enter official launch.
