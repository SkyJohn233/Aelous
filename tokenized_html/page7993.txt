boroughs have made it to the top 50 of best places to live following a quality of life survey six surrey boroughs and districts have been named among the top 50 most desirable places to live in the uk elmbridge came in second place on the annual halifax quality of life survey just behind hart in hampshire with other boroughs spread through the top ranking elmbridge has previously topped the survey four times due to an average weekly earning of £1,104 almost double the uk average of £629 the survey looked at factors including life expectancy crime rates education and weather other surrey boroughs that made it into the top 20 include waverley at number ten mole valley at 19 and surrey heath at 20 guildford came in at 23 reigate banstead at 27 woking in 36th place and epsom ewell at 48
