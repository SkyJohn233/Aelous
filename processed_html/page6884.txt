Harsh Rajput - Wikipedia Harsh Rajput  (born 5 January 1988) is an Indian television actor from Navsari, Gujarat [ 1 ]  who has starred in many television shows and films. He made his television debut with  Dharti Ka Veer Yodha Prithviraj Chauhan  on  StarPlus . He also starred in shows like  Dharam Veer ,  Hitler Didi  and  Crazy Stupid Ishq  where he landed his first lead role. Harsh has also acted in several episodic shows such as  Pyaar Tune Kya Kiya  and  Yeh Hai Aashiqui . He is currently playing the lead role of Ansh Rathod in  StarPlus ' supernatural show  Nazar , produced by  4 Lions Films . His challenging role in the show has helped him establish himself in the television industry.
