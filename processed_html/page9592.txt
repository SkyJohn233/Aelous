Ruston's Kasten Furr (Photo: Submitted photo) West Monroe and Ruston athletes captured top honors in the District 2-5A All-District baseball team recently released by the coaches. Rebel senior Jonathan Snuggs was tabbed as Pitcher of the Year while junior Chase Dejean shared Player of the Year honors with Ruston junior Kasten Furr. The Rebels won the district at 7-3, while Ruston and Natchitoches Central tied for second at 6-3. All three of those schools lost in the first round of the LHSAA Class 5A state baseball playoffs. The district s first team included 24 players with representatives from every team in the league. Additional Bearcats on the first team included pitcher Graham Hanes, third baseman Sam Baldwin and designated hitter CJ Willis, an LSU signee. The Rebel representatives included catcher Carson Jones, outfielders Cayden Pierce, Wes Tatum and Kent Berry, along with utility player Tom Biggs.
