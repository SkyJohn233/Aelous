Liz McDaid - Wikipedia Liz McDaid  is a South African activist who is the "Eco-Justice Lead" for the Southern African Faith Communities' Environment Institute (SAFCEI). [ 1 ]  Along with  Makoma Lekalakala , she was awarded the 2018  Goldman Environmental Prize  for the African region for their work on using the courts to stop a Russian-South African nuclear deal in 2017. [ 2 ]  In 2018 McDaid and Lekalakala received the Nick Steele Memorial Award for their work in winning a crucial court case to halt plans by the South African government to proceed with a national nuclear build programme.
