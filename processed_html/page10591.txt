Bill Belichick on hand in Amherst to watch UMass women &# x27;s lacrosse defeat Holy Cross, 15-6 - masslive.com See more photos from the game » New England Patriots head coach Bill Belichick was at UMass &# x27; McGuirk Stadium on Wednesday to watch the UMass women &# x27;s lacrosse team score a 15-6 victory over Holy Cross. Belichick &# x27;s daughter, Amanda Belichick, is an assistant coach for the Minutewomen. UMass was led by the offense of sophomore Jesse O &# x27;Donnell, who scored five goals. Jackie Lyons netted three, and Haley Smith and Lauren Terracciano added two apiece. The Minutewomen benefited from a fast start, as Danielle Pelletier scored her first goal of the season just 58 seconds into the game. UMass took a 7-1 lead into the half. Goaltender Katie Florence picked up the victory in net. The Minutewomen evened their record at 1-1, after losing to No. 1 ranked Northwestern on February 6.
