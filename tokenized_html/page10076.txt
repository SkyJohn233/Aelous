about us òlàjú art group òlàjú art group was established in 2015 by founder and director obafemi ogunleye as a member-driven platform to advance the development of african arts culture the group is comprised of artists curators collectors and members of the general public with an interest in cross-cultural exchange centered on pan-african heritage our mission is to raise awareness of critical issues concerning west africa and beyond through the use of contemporary art our flagship program is the african market festival which is a one-day celebration of contemporary african arts and culture that takes place annually in san antonio tx in addition to the festival we partner with organizations to develop education-based programming for more information please send a message to info olajuartgroup.org follow us on social media olajuartgroup © copyright the olaju art group 2016
