Four Burrows Solar Park | Our Renewable Energy Projects | Low Carbon North-west of Truro, Four Burrows is a ground-mounted, fixed-tilt solar PV plant on 41 acres of ex-grazing and arable land. With managed hedgerows providing habitats for birds, butterflies, bats and small mammals, the site ensures negligible impact on the EU-protected breeding habits of local skylarks.
