East San Marco | whitehallpartners East San Marco East San Marco is a mixed use project located in the heart of San Marco in Jacksonville, FL. East San Marco will offer 45,000 sq. ft. of ground level retail including a Publix grocery store with 280 multi-family units above and adjacent to the retail. The project is scheduled to break ground Fall 2016 for a Spring 2018 opening. Copyright   2019 whitehallpartners. All Rights Reserved.
