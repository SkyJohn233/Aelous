Om kakor (cookies) Stockholm Hundmässa Utskrift från www.skk.se Copyright Svenska Kennelklubben Box 771, 191 27 Sollentuna Svenska Kennelklubben (The Swedish Kennel Club) welcomes all exhibitors and visitors to a pleasant and exciting weekend just outside of Stockholm. Visitors will be able to watch some of Sweden's best dogs and handlers compete in  junior handling, rally obedience and freestyle. As usual there is trade exhibition in connection with the dog show, where you can shop for both you and your dog. The location of our venue Stockholmsmässan is south of Stockholm, with easy access to highway E4. The address is Mässvägen 1, Älvsjö, Sweden.
