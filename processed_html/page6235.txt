Assessing the Role of Conceptual Knowledge in an Anti-phishing Educational Game - IEEE Conference Publication Skip to Main Content Phishing is a type of identity theft that affects websites, in which unsuspecting individuals are deceived into sending confidential and sensitive information to a third party website as it fraudulently pretends to be another. Typically, an attack presents an individual with a hyperlink which appears to be associated with a website they are familiar with. but is actually associated with another.
