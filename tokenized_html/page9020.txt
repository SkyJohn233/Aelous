future-focused goal-directed and focuses on solutions rather than on the problems that brought clients to seek therapy the miracle question is often embraced when using this form of therapy it involves envisioning what your life would be like if the problem that brought you to therapy would be gone it is used as a building block at first with small achievable goals set in place to help motivate the client to produce solution states the next step is to identify the most recent times when the client has had small pieces of miracles called exceptions and get them to repeat these
