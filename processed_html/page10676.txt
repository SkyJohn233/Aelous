Olivier Pierre - The Arthur Conan Doyle Encyclopedia Olivier Pierre (19 october 1953 - march 2005) was a French actor who played the Director of the Louvre in episode  The Final Problem  of the TV series  Sherlock Holmes  with  Jeremy Brett  as Sherlock Holmes. He also played Mr. Hampton in 1987 in TV movie  The Return of Sherlock Holmes  with  Michael Pennington  as Sherlock Holmes.
