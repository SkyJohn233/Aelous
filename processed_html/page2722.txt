Glendale Profile | Glendale CA | Population, Crime, Map Profile Real Estate Weather Hotels California > Glendale Profile Glendale, CA, population 196,882, is locatedin California's Los Angeles county,about 4.1 miles from  Burbank  and 6.3 miles from  Los Angeles . Through the 90's Glendale's population has grown by about 8%.It is estimated that in the first 5 years of the past decade the population of Glendale has grown by about 3%. Since 2005 Glendale's population has stayed about the same. Glendale's property crime levels tend to be lower than California's average level.The same data shows violent crime levels in Glendale tend to be much lower than California's average level.
