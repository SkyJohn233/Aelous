The home Access Point lite (hAP lite) is an ideal little device for your apartment, house or office. It supports button triggered WPS, for the convenience of not typing a complicated password when somebody wants to have wireless internet access, and can also be told to change to cAP mode and join a CAPsMAN centrally managed network by the push of a button. Of course, the device runs RouterOS with all the features, bandwidth shaping, firewall, user access control and many others. The hAP lite is equipped with a powerful 650MHz CPU, 32MB RAM, dual chain 2.4GHz onboard wireless, four Fast Ethernet ports and a RouterOS L4 license.
