A Note about Ask Banner - First-Year Handbook - Vassar College Vassar Search button Close Menu Ask Banner  is a link at the bottom of the Vassar homepage under  Community  that will give you access to a wide range of important information. The  General Information  link on the Ask Banner site will allow you to view the online schedule of classes as well as the employee and student directories.
