Sredne-Nevskiy Shipyard, JSC - » SN Shipyard — the winner of the National prize “Golden idea – 2014” Saturday, in Moscow took place the solemn ceremony of awarding the laureates of the National prize Golden idea  for merits and achievements in military-technical cooperation of Russia with foreign States .  The winner of one of the prizes was Sredne-Nevsky shipyard (part of USC) . For contribution to raising competitiveness of military products second prize awarded to the author team for providing technical assistance in installation ,  commissioning and handover of property and equipment for boats of project TP-400 , built by a foreign customer.
