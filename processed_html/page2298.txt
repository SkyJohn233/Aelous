AMBASSADOR OF LITHUANIA IN BELGIUM H. E. MR. GEDIMINAS VARVUOLIS AND MINISTER OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS OF TOGO H. E. MR. ROBERT DUSSEY MEETING | News | Ministry of Foreign Affairs On 3 December 2013 Ambassador of Lithuania in Belgium H.E. Mr. Gediminas Varvuolis held a meeting with Minister of Foreign Affairs of Togo H.E. Mr. Robert Dussey. The meeting focused on development of bilateral relations, challenges of membership in the United Nations Security Council and on Lithuania s Presidency of the EU Council. H.E. Minister R. Dussey thanked for the support to the Western African countries, presented possibilities of economic cooperation with Togo
