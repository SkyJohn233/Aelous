voyager telescope-maksutov-telescope For many amateur astronomers portability is an  important factor in their telescopes. With their complex optical design,  including both lenses and mirrors, the compact Maksutov-Cassegrain telescopes  are ideally suited for those amateurs. At the same time image quality is very  good. The optical design of our compact    telescopes is called a Maksutov-Cassegrain. This design utilizes mirrors and    a corrector lens, resulting in a compact design with a very good optical    performance.
