Palliative  &  Supportive Care of Nantucket |  Internet Resources A life enhancement program for persons facing life-threatening illness Funded by the  Palliative  &  Supportive Care of Nantucket Foundation , the Palliative  &  Supportive Care Program is operated as a department of the  Nantucket Cottage Hospital , which is an affiliate of  Massachusetts General Hospital , and a member of  Partners HealthCare . Palliative  &  Supportive Care of Nantucket is a specialized health care program dedicated to providing excellent physical, psychological, social, and spiritual care to persons with life-threatening illness and their families.
