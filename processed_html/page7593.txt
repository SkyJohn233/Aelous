UBS bets on ONGC on crude price reversal, share buyback; sees 57% upside - Moneycontrol.com Global research firm UBS has maintained a buy call on  ONGC  with a target of Rs 240. It implies a potential upside of 57 percent from last traded price. The brokerage highlighted that the stock took a beating due to soaring crude, and it could now reverse with fall in those prices. ONGC Videsh’s consolidated profit during the first half of the current financial year was up almost nine time on a yearly basis on higher crude oil price, it added. Further, share buyback and stable crude oil prices should improve investors' sentiments. The stock has lost over 2 percent in the past one month, while in the past three days, it has fallen over 2.5 percent. At the close of market hours, Oil and Natural Gas Corporation was quoting at Rs 152.60, down Rs 1.80, or 1.17 percent, on the BSE.
