National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) Job Notification:  National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has invited applications for the post of Site Engineer. The eligible candidates can apply to the post through the prescribed format on or before 03 October 2018. Important Date: Last Date of Application: 03 October 2018 National Highways Authority of India Vacancy Details  Site Engineer: 03 Posts  Eligibility Criteria for Site Engineer Job  Educational Qualification:  Degree in Civil Engineering from any recognized University with more than one year experience in Govt./PSU/Autonomous in Road and Bridge works. Age Limit  30 years Experience  3 years experience in preconstruction Activity (e.g. Land Acquisition  &  Utility Shifting etc.) Construction/Maintenance of National Highway and preparation of estimate for Highway/Bridge etc. Candidate having experience in National Highway Project will be preferred. How to Apply for the NHAI Site Engineer Posts Jobs 2018 Eligible candidates can apply to the post through the prescribed format and send the applications along with other necessary documents to  Project Director. National Highway Authority of India. Project Implementation Unit Godhra Bamroli Road Deepali Society, Gujarat-389001  on or before 03 October 2018.
