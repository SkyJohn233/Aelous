Wilfred Lytell - Biography - IMDb               Wilfred Lytell was born on October 16, 1891 in New York City, New York, USA. He was an actor, known for  Heliotrope  (1920),  Thunderbolts of Fate  (1919) and  The Wolf's Fangs  (1922). He was married to Jessie Mueller. He died on September 10, 1954 in Salem, New York.
