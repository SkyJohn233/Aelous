Sanofi-Aventis to lay off 1,700 in U.S. - lehighvalleylive.com Sanofi-Aventis SA plans to eliminate 1,700 jobs in its U.S. pharmaceutical business, or about 25 percent of its American work force. The world’s fourth-biggest drugmaker announced the pending job cuts Friday. Job losses will primarily hit sales representatives around the country and administrative staff at in Bridgewater, N.J. About 1,400 sales staff will be laid off, as well as about 300 staff in various administrative jobs, Sanofi-Aventis spokesman Jack Cox said. Any impact on subsidiary company , which makes vaccines in Swiftwater, Monroe County, wasn’t clear Friday. A company statement read: About 2,000 people work at the Swiftwater facility. Decisions on which Sanofi-Aventis employees will go are to be completed by mid-December. Read the Associated Press story : .
