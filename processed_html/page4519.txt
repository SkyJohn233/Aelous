NIU, Leader for Smart Electric Scooters in Italy | Design Diffusion During  EICMA 2018  – Europe’s top show dedicated to two-wheelers –  Chinese brand NIU , the world’s #1 company for  smart electric scooters , presented its complete range, including the products that will be available from 2019. All NIU models are designed i n collaboration with Bosch, Panasonic and Vodafone  to create the new generation of smart electric scooters and meet the increasingly widespread needs of European customers. After selling  430,000 bikes  over the first 4 years and opening  700 stores across China  in over  150 cities , NIU is revolutionizing its portfolio with two new models that will be available from next year. They are  NGT  and  M+ , equipped with a specially tailored  Bosch motor  with a nominal power output of 3,000w and 1,200w respectively, able to reach a top speed of 70 km/h, and with  greater autonomy  than the previous models. The new scooters by NIU are equipped with  two batteries  that make it possible to  ride for 100km  on a single charge. But that’s not all: the company has recently entered the B2B market with the N-Sharing model.  The N-Sharing scooter  has the same characteristics as the N series (NIU’s best seller), but it is equipped with dual Panasonic 60V 29Ah batteries for a 3.48 KW output. Thanks to the collaboration with many companies in different countries and cities of the world, such as  GoUrban  in Vienna,  Cabify Movo  in Madrid and  Europcar  Scooty in Brussels and Antwerp,  NIU goes on supporting sharing economy all over the world  through targeted partnerships and smart technologies.  A project that, according to  Michael Castellano , EU Sales Representative of NIU, will soon arrive in Italy inevitably raising much interest since, to drive these models, i t will be enough to be 16 and have a riding license.
