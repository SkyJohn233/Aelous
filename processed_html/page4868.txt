Statin Nation - BOOK REVIEW - Fat and Cholesterol Don’t... | Facebook BOOK REVIEW - Fat and Cholesterol Don’t Cause Heart Attacks and Statins are Not the Solution.  The book is a collection of chapters written by 21 members of THINCS (The International Network of Cholesterol Skeptics) and edited by Paul J. Rosch, Professor of Medicine and Psychiatry at New York Medical College. Each author is a world-renowned expert on the subjects covered in their respective chapters. The book is also a tribute to Dr. Uffe Ravnskov, who has worked tirelessly for many years to expose the cholesterol myths - publishing more than 150 scientific papers challenging the dogma surrounding the causes of heart disease.
